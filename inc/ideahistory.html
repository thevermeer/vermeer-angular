Foreshadow Demand Forecasting Suite
Demand the Future
Foreshadow is an enterprise-scale demand forecasting suite built from core modules and customized business rules and interfaces. Fs Server is the heart of the solution featuring 3 paralell methodologies for forecasting demand. Based on implementation, Fs Server performs data parsing, statistical optimization and normalization, forecasting and supply-chain scheduling. The Foreshadow Suite was implemented by BMO Bank of Montreal from 2006-2011 for use in optimizing currency suppy to ATM networks. This implementation forecasted usage patterns at over 2000 cashpoints nationwide.

Retailor.NET Cashroom Automation
Custom-fit Cashroom Automation
Designed around a core suite for transaction processing and accouting, Retailor.NET is built for vendors of bill and coin counters, cheque image readers and cashroom automation technologies to package to retail customers. The software deploys customer-specific hardware configurations against business rules to automate general ledger reporting and fortify loss and fraud prevention from cash processing. The greater goal of the software is to assist retailers in performing end-of-day cash processing in-store, eliminating extensive bank service fees.
